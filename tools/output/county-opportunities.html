MLI = Market Liquidity Index (0–100): how fast a market absorbs & sells — Z-scored from Months of inventory 40% · Days on market 40% · Sale-to-list 20%. This is the metric land flippers sell on.
Score = Opportunity score (0–100), liquidity-first: MLI 60% · Population growth 15% · Construction intensity 10% · Affordable entry 15%. Price/sales momentum is shown for context but earns 0 weight — our backtest showed it doesn't predict outcomes.
A where-to-look signal, not a buy signal. Always run individual parcels through the Deal Analyzer + diligence checklist. Currently uses residential data as a free proxy for land demand; run backtest.py to see how well the MLI predicts forward liquidity.
